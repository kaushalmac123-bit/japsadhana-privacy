Account Deletion

Effective Date: April 6, 2026

Your Right to Privacy

At Jaap Sadhana, we believe you should have absolute control over your spiritual data. We provide a transparent and straightforward way to delete your account and all associated records.

Whether you choose to move on or restart your journey, we ensure your data is handled with the same sanctity it was recorded with.

How to Initiate Deletion

Soft Delete Policy (30 Days)

When you initiate a deletion request, your account enters a 30-day "Soft Delete" period. During this window, your data is preserved but your profile is immediately deactivated.

To delete your account, follow these steps in the app:

  • Open Jaap Sadhana and go to the Profile tab.
  • Tap on Settings (gear icon).
  • Select Delete Account at the bottom of the list.
  • Confirm your choice in the security prompt.

Account Restoration

We understand that spiritual journeys can have pauses. If you change your mind within the 30-day restoration window, you can easily recover your account.

  • How to Restore: Simply log back into the app using your original authentication method (Google or Apple Sign-In).
  • Data Recovery: All your streaks, logs, and group memberships will be instantly restored as if you never left.

Note: After the 30-day period expires, the restoration window closes and the data becomes permanently inaccessible.

What Data is Removed?

Upon final deletion (after 30 days), the following information is permanently purged:

  • Your personal profile (Name, Email, Profile Photo).
  • Comprehensive Jaap logs and Sadhana history.
  • Community rankings and Leaderboard presence.
  • Group memberships and shared spiritual contributions.
  • Calculated milestones and earned badges.
