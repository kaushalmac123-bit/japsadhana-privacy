Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 3, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Jaap Sadhana. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your data when you use our mobile application and services.

Jaap Sadhana is designed as a digital sanctuary for spiritual practitioners. While we provide advanced features like global leaderboards and group synchronization, we strictly prioritize the sanctity and privacy of your spiritual journey.

2. Data Collection

To provide high-performance features, we collect several types of information from our users:

A. Personal Information

Authentication Name, Email, Profile Photo URL, and Auth Provider (Google/Apple).
Social Context User-configured display name and bio for community features.

B. Activity & Sadhana Data

Jaap Logs Individual chanting records, counts, timestamps, and mantra sources.
Progress Stats Streaks (current/best), total counts, and achievement milestones.
Custom Content Personalized mantra names, themes, and floating text history.

C. Device & Technical Data

Identifiers Unique Firebase/Supabase User IDs and Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens.
Device Metadata Device type (Android/iOS), model name, timezone, and language preferences.

3. Third-Party Services

We use industry-standard cloud providers to ensure data reliability and security:

  • Supabase: For our core PostgreSQL database, real-time data synchronization, and serverless Edge Functions.
  • Firebase (Google): For native authentication (Google Sign-In), Cloud Messaging (Push Notifications), and Cloud Storage for profile media.
  • Syncfusion & Hive: For premium analytical charting and encrypted local data persistence.

4. How We Use Data

We process your information for the following specific purposes:

  • Core Functionality: To synchronize your progress across multiple devices.
  • Community Energy: To display your ranking (if opted-in) in Global and Weekly leaderboards.
  • Group Sadhana: To allow group members to see collective progress and manage memberships.
  • Milestones: To calculate and award badges based on your chanting consistency.
  • Engagement: To send reminders and achievement alerts via push notifications.

5. User Rights & Controls

We believe in giving you absolute control over your profile:

  • Privacy Mode: You can choose to "Hide Profile" in settings to participate in leadboards anonymously or not at all.
  • Access & Correction: You can update your profile information at any time within the App's Profile settings.
  • Data Export: You can view your detailed logs via the Statistics dashboard.

6. Children's Privacy

Jaap Sadhana is a spiritual tool and does not target children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal identifiable information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately delete this from our servers.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

Mandatory Disclosure

In compliance with modern app store regulations (Play Store & App Store 2024), we provide a clear and permanent path for account and data deletion.

You may request the permanent deletion of your account and all associated data (Jaap logs, group memberships, and profile info) at any time. This action is irreversible.

How to Delete Your Data:

  1. In-App: Go to Profile Settings > "Delete Account" to initiate immediate automated deletion.
  2. Via Email: Send a request from your registered email address to dev2applitz2020@gmail.com with the subject "Data Deletion Request".

Upon request, all PII (Personally Identifiable Information) is removed from our production databases within 7 days.
